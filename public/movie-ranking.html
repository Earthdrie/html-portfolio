The Best Movies According to Logan M. Andrus

My top 4 Movies of all-time.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Peter Jacksons Lord of the rings is a timeless classic and the greatest cinematic achievement in film making history. Epic battles.

Braveheart

Epic battles, emotional scenes that stick with you for the rest of your life, Passionate ideals of the main character that truly make the audience feel they are in the shoes of William Wallace.

American History X

A deeply emotional film that tells the story of an elder brother through the the eyes of a younger brother. The elder brother must stop his younger brother from going down the path he went down.

Django Unchained

This story focuses on Django a former slave turned bounty hunter. Django travels they American South in search of his enslaved wife whom he yearns to free. Great acting and dialog. Gory action that will leave you at the edge of your seat.

Come and See

A WWII foregin film set in Belorussia. It is a horriffic and gruesome war film that focuses on the Nazi invasion and occupation of Belorussia. A story told through the eyes of a young boy whom joins the resistance to fight the invaders but does not understand the horrors that await him and the extent to the atrocities his enemies commit. This is truly a brutally emotional film that is not for the faint of heart.
